# Client Background

## Client Name

Northwind Retail Group

## Industry

Retail and e-commerce

## Company Overview

Northwind Retail Group is a mid-sized retail company operating both physical stores and an online sales platform. The company sells consumer products across multiple regions and manages customer orders, inventory, payments, store operations, and delivery workflows.

## Business Context

The company has grown quickly, but its data systems have not matured at the same pace. Sales data, customer data, inventory data, and delivery data are spread across multiple systems. Reporting teams rely on manual extracts, spreadsheets, and disconnected dashboards.

Leadership wants to modernize the company’s data platform using Azure cloud services. The goal is to create a trusted analytics environment that supports near-real-time operational visibility, executive reporting, data governance, and future AI/ML initiatives.

## Simulated Role

Azure Data Engagement & Technical Lead

## Role Responsibilities

In this simulated engagement, the lead is responsible for:

- Understanding business goals and stakeholder pain points
- Defining the target-state Azure data architecture
- Organizing the work into epics, features, user stories, and sprint tasks
- Coordinating technical workstreams across data engineering, BI, governance, and cloud
- Delivering batch and real-time data pipelines
- Designing analytics-ready data models
- Preparing Power BI reporting requirements
- Defining governance, quality, and operational support practices
- Creating an AI/ML readiness roadmap
