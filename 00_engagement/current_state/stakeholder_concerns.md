# Stakeholder Concerns

## Project

RetailPulse Azure Data Engagement Platform

## Purpose

This document captures stakeholder concerns for the Northwind Retail Group data modernization engagement.

The goal is to understand what each stakeholder group needs from the platform and what risks or expectations must be managed.

---

## Stakeholder Concern Matrix

| Stakeholder | Main Concern | What They Need From the Platform |
|---|---|---|
| CIO | Modernization, scalability, cost control, delivery risk | Clear roadmap, Azure architecture, cost visibility, governance |
| VP Sales | Revenue visibility, customer trends, sales performance | Trusted sales KPIs and executive dashboard |
| Operations Manager | Order delays, fulfillment bottlenecks, cancellation monitoring | Near-real-time order and fulfillment dashboard |
| Finance Manager | Revenue accuracy and reconciliation | Consistent revenue definitions and finance-aligned metrics |
| Inventory Manager | Stockouts, overstock, product availability | Inventory dashboard and product-level visibility |
| BI Team | Clean data model, dashboard performance, KPI definitions | Gold layer tables and governed semantic model |
| Data Engineering Team | Source system complexity, data quality, pipeline reliability | Clear schemas, data contracts, ingestion standards |
| Security/Governance Team | Access control, sensitive data, auditability | RBAC, Key Vault, lineage, data quality rules |
| Support/Ops Team | Monitoring, failures, handoff clarity | Runbook, alerts, escalation path, operational ownership |
| Executive Sponsor | Business value, timeline, scope, risk | Status reporting, roadmap, milestone visibility |

---

## Major Cross-Cutting Concerns

### 1. Trust in Data

Stakeholders need confidence that dashboards reflect approved definitions and reliable data.

### 2. Delivery Visibility

Leadership needs to know what is complete, what is blocked, what is next, and what decisions are needed.

### 3. Cost Control

Cloud resources must be deployed carefully with budgets, tags, and cleanup policies.

### 4. Security and Access

Sensitive customer and sales data must be protected using least-privilege access.

### 5. Operational Readiness

The platform must include monitoring, support ownership, and escalation procedures.

### 6. AI/ML Readiness

Leadership is interested in AI/ML and LLM use cases, but the data foundation must be reliable first.

---

## Leadership Interpretation

Capturing stakeholder concerns allows the Engagement & Technical Lead to align technical delivery with business expectations.

It also helps prevent scope confusion, late escalations, and solutions that solve technical problems without addressing business needs.
