# Business Problem

Northwind Retail Group currently lacks a unified, governed, and timely data platform.

## Current Challenges

1. Sales, customer, inventory, and delivery data are stored in separate systems.
2. Reports are produced manually using spreadsheets and disconnected extracts.
3. Executives do not have a trusted single view of revenue, orders, fulfillment, and inventory.
4. Operations teams cannot monitor order activity in near real time.
5. Data quality issues are discovered late, often after reports have already been shared.
6. KPI definitions are inconsistent across departments.
7. There is limited governance around data ownership, access control, and lineage.
8. The company wants to explore AI/ML use cases but lacks a reliable data foundation.

## Business Impact

These issues create:

- Delayed decision-making
- Conflicting reports across departments
- Poor visibility into fulfillment and delivery delays
- Difficulty tracking revenue and operational performance
- Increased manual effort for analysts
- Reduced trust in reporting
- Limited readiness for advanced analytics and AI/ML

## Engagement Objective

Design and deliver an Azure-based data modernization platform that provides:

- Batch ingestion for core business data
- Real-time streaming for order and fulfillment events
- Bronze, Silver, and Gold data layers
- Trusted analytics models for reporting
- Power BI dashboards for executives and operations
- Data governance and quality controls
- Operational monitoring and support processes
- AI/ML readiness roadmap
