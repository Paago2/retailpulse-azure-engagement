# Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder | Role | Primary Concerns | Engagement Need |
|---|---|---|---|
| CIO | Executive Sponsor | Modernization, scalability, cloud strategy, cost control | Clear roadmap, risk visibility, executive reporting |
| VP of Sales | Business Leader | Revenue visibility, sales trends, customer behavior | Trusted sales dashboards and KPIs |
| Operations Manager | Business Leader | Order fulfillment, delivery delays, operational bottlenecks | Near-real-time operational dashboards |
| Finance Manager | Business Leader | Revenue accuracy, reconciliation, financial reporting | Consistent revenue metrics and governed reporting |
| Inventory Manager | Business Leader | Stock availability, low inventory, demand trends | Inventory analytics and exception reporting |
| Data Engineering Team | Technical Team | Pipelines, data quality, architecture, maintainability | Clear requirements, data contracts, technical backlog |
| BI Team | Technical Team | Semantic models, dashboards, DAX measures, performance | Clean curated data and standardized KPI definitions |
| Security/Governance Team | Control Function | Access control, compliance, data ownership, lineage | Governance framework and access matrix |
| Support Team | Operations | Monitoring, failures, incident response | Runbooks, alerts, and operational documentation |
