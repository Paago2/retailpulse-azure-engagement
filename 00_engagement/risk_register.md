# Risk Register

| Risk ID | Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| R001 | Source data quality is poor | Reports may be inaccurate | High | Add validation rules and data quality dashboard | Data Engineering Lead |
| R002 | Stakeholders have conflicting KPI definitions | Low trust in dashboards | High | Create KPI glossary and approval process | Engagement Lead |
| R003 | Real-time pipeline cost grows unexpectedly | Budget risk | Medium | Start with controlled event volume and monitor cost | Cloud Architect |
| R004 | Azure access or permissions delay implementation | Delivery delay | Medium | Define RBAC and access needs early | Security/Governance Lead |
| R005 | Scope expands beyond initial delivery | Timeline risk | High | Manage backlog priorities and change control | Engagement Lead |
| R006 | Power BI performance issues occur | Poor user adoption | Medium | Use star schema, optimized DAX, aggregations, and model tuning | BI Lead |
| R007 | Lack of operational ownership after delivery | Support gaps | Medium | Create runbook and handoff documentation | Support Lead |
| R008 | AI/ML use cases start before data foundation is ready | Poor model results | Medium | Prioritize data quality and governance before AI/ML scaling | AI/ML Lead |
