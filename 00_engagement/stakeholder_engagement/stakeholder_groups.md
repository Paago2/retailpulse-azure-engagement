# Stakeholder Groups

## Project

RetailPulse Azure Data Engagement Platform

## Purpose

This document identifies the stakeholder groups involved in the RetailPulse Azure data modernization engagement.

The goal is to define who needs to be informed, consulted, involved in decisions, or responsible for delivery.

---

| Stakeholder Group | Example Role | Interest in Project | Engagement Need |
|---|---|---|---|
| Executive Sponsors | CIO, VP Operations, VP Sales | Business outcomes, cost, risk, roadmap | Executive updates and decision points |

| AI/ML Strategy Stakeholders | AI Lead, Data Scientist, Innovation Sponsor | Future ML, LLM, RAG, analytics automation | Use case prioritization and governance alignment |

## RACI Overview

RACI means:

- Responsible: person or team doing the work
- Accountable: person who owns the final outcome
- Consulted: person or team providing input
- Informed: person or team kept updated

| Workstream | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Engagement roadmap | Engagement Lead | Executive Sponsor | Business Owners, Tech Lead | All stakeholders |
| Azure architecture | Cloud/Data Architect | Tech Lead | Security, Data Engineering | Executive Sponsor |
| Batch ingestion | Data Engineering Team | Tech Lead | Business Owners | Engagement Lead |
| Real-time streaming | Data Engineering Team | Tech Lead | Operations Manager, Security | Engagement Lead |
| Power BI reporting | BI Team | BI Lead | Business Owners, Governance | Executive Sponsor |
| Data governance | Governance Lead | Security/Governance Sponsor | BI, Data Engineering | Executive Sponsor |

## Leadership Interpretation

Stakeholder management is not only about sending updates.

It is about making sure the right people are involved at the right time, decisions are documented, risks are escalated early, and delivery remains aligned to business outcomes.
